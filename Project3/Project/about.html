About Whisker Haven

Welcome to Whisker Haven, your one-stop shop for all things pet-related! At Whisker Haven, we believe in providing top-notch care and products for your furry, feathered, and scaly friends. Our mission is to create a haven where pets are pampered, loved, and cared for just like family.


Our Story


Founded in 2020, Whisker Haven started as a small pet supply store. Over the years, we have grown into a comprehensive pet store offering a wide range of services including grooming, veterinary care, and adoption services. Our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction has made us a trusted name in the community.
